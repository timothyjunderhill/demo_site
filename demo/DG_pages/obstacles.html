Objects of Dash Galaxy

Objects

While exploring the galaxy, a player will come across many different type of objects. Many of these objects will cause damage to the player, such as an asteroid. On the other hand, certain objects may prove advantageous such as a shield. Depending on the game mode and world type, different types of objects will appear.
