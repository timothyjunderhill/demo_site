Dash Galaxy

Dash Galaxy is an IOS game developed and published (Apple App Store) in 2018. In Dash Galaxy, the player will guide a spaceship through an ever changing galaxy. As more of the galaxy is explored, the player's score will increase. The player will start off with three lives; if the user hits a rock or asteroid, they will lose a life, if they travel to another planet they will gain one.
