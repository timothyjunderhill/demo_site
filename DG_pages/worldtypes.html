World Types of Dash Galaxy

Gamemodes

Two main game modes exist: space mode and planet mode. In space mode, the player will have to dodge asteroids and other spaceships. In planet mode, a planet will be generated with unique threats to challenge the player.

World Types

Each planet is created based off a world type. Each world type has a unique chance of being generated. Six world types are commonly generated: Forest, Ocean, Lava, Desert, Candy, and Rare World. Each world type has unique threats and obstacles to dodge.
