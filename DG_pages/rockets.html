Rockets of Dash Galaxy

Rockets

Dash Galaxy has nine available rockets for a player to use. All of these rockets are locked at the beginning of a new game except for the default rocket, Ghost Wing. Rockets are currently unlocked by being purchased with gems at the store. However, a potential future feature would add another step and make unlocking rockets more difficult.

While there are nine rockets in the game, only Ghost Wing has damaged versions of it in relation to the number of lives a player has. Adding this feature for all rockets is another planned feature.

Gems are found in both space and on planets but are much more common on Rare Worlds. Gems are found in varying color but all increase the score the same amount. Future versions may see different colors with different score values.
