Planned Features for Dash Galaxy

Faster Gameplay

Because Dash Galaxy is still in its first version, it has a lot of room for improvement. One of the biggest ways Dash Galaxy can be improved is by making changes that will make the game run faster. Many of these changes are being worked on before the next release version.

Smaller

Along with being faster, the next version of Dash Galaxy will take up significantly less memory. It is expected that the next version will be 1/4th the current size.

More World Types

Additional world types, and their matching obstacles, are planned. Certain world types include: Mesa, Dark Desert, Shadow, Nebula, and Ruins.

Graver Threats

Enemy Ships will have more abilities, and will now appear on Planets in the form of Desert and Snow Crawlers.

Greater Discoveries

Rockets will now have to be discovered before they can be purchased. A rocket will be unlocked when the player comes across a specific rocket's rocket parts in the galaxy. Rockets parts will be found on planets, and much more commonly in the Ruins world types.

Better Health and Abilities System

Different rockets will now have different health levels. Additionally each rocket will have a special ability ranging from regenerating health to immunity from enemy fire.

Sound / Music

Sound effects will eventually be implemented into the game. Additionally, tracks are being produced on Fl Studios to be included the next release version.
